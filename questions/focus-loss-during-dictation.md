# Focus loss during dictation

**Asked:** 14/04/26
**Slug:** `focus-loss-during-dictation`

## The question

When using live voice typing that's supposed to insert text at the cursor position, it's very easy — while waiting for the transcription to arrive — to move into another workflow context (switch windows, click into something else).

The standard solution is to also place the text on the clipboard. But what other alternatives are there? For example: when you return to the original window, the text is pasted where it was originally intended to be.

What's the design space here?
